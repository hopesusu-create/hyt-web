Common inquiries for North American Buyers

How does HYT Packaging ensure quality for overseas shipping? ↓

We use reinforced five-layer corrugated cartons with internal dividers and plastic palletizing. Every shipment undergoes a 3-point inspection before leaving our facility.

Can HYT help with North American port logistics? ↓

Yes. We offer DDP/CIF terms to major ports like Long Beach, New York, and Savannah, including customs clearance assistance for a seamless delivery experience.
